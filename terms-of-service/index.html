Terms of Service

SecureTheCloud Labs provides educational security content for practical learning, architecture review, AI governance, and cloud security decision-making.

Labs content is provided for learning and portfolio development. It must not be used to attack real systems, bypass authorization, access customer data, collect secrets, run live exploit activity, mutate production environments, or claim production enforcement.

All examples, scenarios, and exercises should remain synthetic and bounded. Learners are responsible for using the material lawfully, safely, and only in systems where they have explicit authorization.

SecureTheCloud Labs may update learning paths, pages, examples, and trust boundaries as the platform evolves.
