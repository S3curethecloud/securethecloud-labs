Responsible Disclosure

SecureTheCloud welcomes responsible reports about security issues affecting SecureTheCloud-owned web properties.

Do not test against third-party systems, customer environments, cloud accounts, production workloads, or any system you do not own or have explicit permission to assess. Do not access, copy, store, disclose, or exfiltrate data. Do not perform denial-of-service testing, social engineering, credential attacks, destructive testing, or runtime mutation.

A helpful report should include the affected SecureTheCloud page or asset, a clear description of the issue, safe reproduction steps, impact explanation, and any relevant screenshots that do not expose secrets or personal data.

Reports should remain bounded, non-destructive, and reviewer-safe.
