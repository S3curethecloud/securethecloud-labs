Privacy Policy

SecureTheCloud Labs is an educational platform for cloud security, AI governance, and architecture-focused learning. The Labs site is designed as static educational content with no learner login, no backend exposure, no live enforcement, no customer cloud access, and no runtime mutation.

This privacy posture means the Labs experience should not require customer records, production secrets, credentials, cloud provider access, or sensitive runtime data. Any learning examples should remain synthetic, bounded, and educational.

External platforms, browsers, hosting providers, or linked third-party sites may apply their own privacy practices when you leave SecureTheCloud Labs. Review the destination site before sharing information.

For security or privacy concerns related to this Labs site, use the Responsible Disclosure page.
